About Us

Amour et Grace is a simple yet sophisticated special space for everyone who wants to refresh their mind and body and regain their vitality.

Here, you can enjoy drinks, food, and even sing and dance. Our staff will assist you in providing warm hospitality with a friendly atmosphere.

You can also enjoy our homemade dishes, a fusion of Filipino cuisine and simple Japanese food.
